2. Eligibility Requirements
To donate blood through Life Link, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • Be in good general health
  • Not have donated blood in the last 3 months
  • Not have any transmissible diseases
  • Pass the pre-donation health screening

3. Identity Verification
All users must complete identity verification by submitting valid government-issued identification (citizenship card or passport). This information is used to ensure the safety and security of our blood supply.

4. Medical Screening and Testing
All donated blood will undergo comprehensive testing including but not limited to:

  • Blood group and Rh factor testing
  • HIV screening
  • Hepatitis B and C testing
  • Hemoglobin level testing
  • Other infectious disease screening as required

5. Privacy and Data Protection
We are committed to protecting your personal and medical information. Your data will be:

  • Stored securely and encrypted
  • Used only for blood donation management purposes
  • Shared only with authorized Red Cross personnel
  • Never sold or shared with third parties for marketing

6. Donor Responsibilities
As a donor, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful health information
  • Disclose any medical conditions or risk factors
  • Follow pre-donation and post-donation guidelines
  • Notify us of any adverse reactions after donation
  • Keep your contact information up to date

7. Blood Request Guidelines
When requesting blood, you must:

  • Provide accurate patient information
  • Submit requests through authorized medical facilities
  • Understand that blood availability is not guaranteed
  • Work with Red Cross personnel for fulfillment
